Sourdough

Description

Sourdough Bread is a naturally leavened bread made using a fermented starter rather than commercial yeast.
It has a chewy texture, a slightly tangy flavor, and a crisp crust.
The process takes time but yields an artisanal, deeply satisfying loaf rich in flavor and character.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough starter – 100g
  • Bread flour – 500g
  • Water – 350ml
  • Salt – 10g

Steps

  1. Mix flour and water; let sit for 30 minutes (autolyse).
  2. Add sourdough starter and salt; mix until combined.
  3. Perform stretch and folds every 30 minutes for 2 hours.
  4. Bulk ferment at room temperature for 4–6 hours until doubled.
  5. Shape the dough and place in a floured proofing basket.
  6. Refrigerate overnight for cold proof.
  7. Preheat oven with a Dutch oven inside to 250°C (480°F).
  8. Score and bake the loaf covered for 20 minutes,
    then uncovered for 20–25 minutes until golden brown.
