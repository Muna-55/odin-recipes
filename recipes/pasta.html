Red Sauce Pasta

Description

Red Sauce Pasta is a comforting and flavorful Italian dish made with a tomato-based sauce. It's perfect for a cozy lunch or dinner, offering a balance of tangy tomatoes, sweet onions, and fragrant herbs.
The sauce is slow-cooked with garlic and olive oil to enhance its depth, then tossed with pasta and finished with fresh basil or parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (penne or spaghetti) – 200g
  • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons
  • Onion (chopped) – 1
  • Garlic (minced) – 2 cloves
  • Canned tomatoes – 400g
  • Sugar – 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and pepper – to taste
  • Fresh basil – optional
  • Grated parmesan – optional

Steps

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion until soft.
  3. Add garlic and cook for a minute.
  4. Pour in canned tomatoes, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Toss cooked pasta in the sauce and mix well.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil or parmesan before serving.
